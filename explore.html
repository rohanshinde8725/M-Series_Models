BMW X & M Series Overview

Explore the dynamic and luxurious BMW X and M Series, offering cutting-edge technology, performance, and design.

BMW X Series

BMW X1

The BMW X1 is a luxury compact SUV that delivers impressive agility and practicality.

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 228 HP
  • Torque: 258 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Top Speed: 130 mph

BMW X2

The BMW X2 offers a sportier take on the X1 with a coupe-inspired design and dynamic performance.

  • Engine: 2.0L TwinPower Turbo Inline-4
  • Horsepower: 228 HP
  • Torque: 258 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Top Speed: 143 mph

BMW X3

The BMW X3 is a premium midsize SUV that delivers a perfect balance of power and comfort.

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 / 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 248 HP - 382 HP
  • Torque: 258 lb-ft - 365 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Drivetrain: AWD
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

BMW X4

The BMW X4 is a sportier version of the X3, featuring a coupe-inspired design.

  • Engine: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 / 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6
  • Horsepower: 248 HP - 503 HP
  • Torque: 258 lb-ft - 479 lb-ft
  • Transmission: 8-Speed Automatic
  • Drivetrain: AWD
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

BMW X5

The BMW X5 is a luxurious midsize SUV with impressive performance.

  • Engine: 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 / 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 335 HP - 523 HP
  • Torque: 331 lb-ft - 553 lb-ft
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

BMW X6

The BMW X6 blends SUV capability with coupe-like sportiness.

  • Engine: 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 / 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 335 HP - 617 HP
  • Torque: 331 lb-ft - 553 lb-ft
  • Top Speed: 155 mph

BMW X7

The BMW X7 is the pinnacle of luxury SUVs with spaciousness and power.

  • Engine: 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 / 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 375 HP - 523 HP
  • Torque: 398 lb-ft - 553 lb-ft
  • Top Speed: 155 mph
